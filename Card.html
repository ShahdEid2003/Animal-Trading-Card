Butterfly

An interesting fact about butterflies is that they can taste with their feet!

  • Scientific Name:Danaus plexippus
  • Average Length:Wingspan of about 3.5 inches
  • Lifespan:Generally 2 to 6 weeks.
  • Habitat: Meadows, fields, and gardens.

Butterflies are delicate insects in the order Lepidoptera, sharing it with moths. They have large, colorful wings covered in tiny scales, creating patterns for camouflage, attraction, or warning.
